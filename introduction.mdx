## Overview

Cercle Talents is an AI-powered recruitment CRM that automates your hiring processes. You centralize candidate data, match profiles intelligently, and track applications in real-time. Designed for HR teams and recruitment agencies, it saves time on manual tasks and provides actionable insights through analytics.

With modules for talent pooling, client management, AI assistance, job postings, sourcing, and market monitoring, you streamline your entire recruitment workflow. Start your free 14-day trial without a credit card to experience the benefits immediately.
