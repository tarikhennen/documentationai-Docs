## Prerequisites

Before diving in, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info" title="Requirements">
- A valid email address for account verification
- Access to your email inbox (including spam folder)
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
</Callout>

Cercle Talents is a cloud-based recruitment CRM, so no local installation is required. You'll access everything through your web browser at `https://dashboard.cercletalents.com`.

## Create Your Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Signup Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to the [signup page](https://www.cercle-agents.com/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user-plus">
    Provide your email, name, and cabinet details. Choose your plan (Starter recommended for first-time users).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from Cercle Talents. Click to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
If you don't receive the email, check spam or resend from the login page.
</Callout>

## Complete Onboarding Wizard

After verification, you'll land on the personalized onboarding flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Sector" icon="target">
    Choose your industry: RH, Presse, Tech, Santé, Commerce, or Juridique.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Modules" icon="settings">
    Enable core modules like Vivier de talents and Gestion clients.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Preferences" icon="sliders">
    Define your default matching criteria for candidates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This wizard tailors your dashboard to your recruitment needs.

## Access Dashboard and Modules

Your dashboard provides an overview of candidates, clients, and pipelines.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Vivier de Talents" icon="users">
    Centralize and search your candidate database.
    
    Key actions:
    - Upload CVs via drag-and-drop
    - Use IA assistant for smart matching
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Gestion Clients" icon="briefcase">
    Manage contacts and mandates.
    
    ```
    Example client entry:
    {
      "name": "Client ABC",
      "mandat": "Développeur Senior",
      "status": "Actif"
    }
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Assistant IA" icon="bot">
    Get intelligent sourcing recommendations.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
