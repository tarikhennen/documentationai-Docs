---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: How to resolve dashboard login issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Stuck on login page
- Error: "Unable to load dashboard"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Ensure you are connected and try reloading the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Log out, clear your browser cache, then log in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different browser">
    Switch to Chrome or Firefox to rule out browser-specific issues.
  </Step>
</Steps>
