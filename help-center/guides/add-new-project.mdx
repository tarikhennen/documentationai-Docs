description: Step-by-step: Create a new project in Cercle Talents.
---

## Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Go to `https://app.cercletalents.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Project">
    Find the **New Project** button at the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details">
    Enter your project name and description, then click **Create**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Your Project">
    Your project appears in the list. Click it to start adding docs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
