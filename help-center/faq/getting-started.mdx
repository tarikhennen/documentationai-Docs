## Quick Start

1. Log in to Cercle Talents at `https://app.cercletalents.com`
2. Click the **New Project** button at the top right
3. Enter a name and optional description
4. Click **Create**

Your new project will now appear in your dashboard. You can add documentation by clicking on your project and selecting **Add Page**. For more help, visit the Guides section.
